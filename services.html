My Practice

The Approaches I Incorporate

Every session is tailored to the individual. I draw from a range of evidence-based and holistic modalities to best support your unique journey.

ACT

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy

ACT helps clients develop psychological flexibility by learning to accept difficult thoughts and emotions while committing to actions that align with their values and create meaningful change.

IFS

Internal Family Systems

IFS is a compassionate and empowering approach that explores the different "parts" within us. It helps individuals heal emotional wounds, build self-awareness, and create greater inner harmony and balance.

Person-Centred

Person-Centred Therapy

Developed by Carl Rogers, this approach provides a safe, supportive, and non-judgmental environment where clients can explore their experiences, discover their strengths, and move toward personal growth and self-acceptance.

EFT

Emotion-Focused Therapy

EFT helps clients better understand, process, and regulate their emotions. By working with emotions in a safe and supportive way, individuals can develop healthier coping strategies, improve relationships, and foster emotional resilience.

Integrative

Holistic and Integrative Practice

My work also incorporates holistic principles, recognizing the connection between the mind, body, and emotions. This allows me to tailor each session to the individual, creating a personalized therapeutic experience that supports lasting change and overall wellbeing.

Specialised Support

Perinatal & Postpartum Support

Specialised support for women during pregnancy, postpartum and motherhood — and for couples navigating the changes that come with becoming parents. Sessions are tailored to your needs, gently combining talking therapy with body-based practices.

FAQ

Common Questions

What types of services do you offer?

Gloria offers individual counselling, clinical hypnotherapy, couples therapy, vibroacoustic therapy, sound healing, breathwork workshops, master classes, and 5-session wellness packages — in-person in North Brisbane and online.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. Online sessions are available via WhatsApp to clients anywhere in Australia and worldwide, in English, Portuguese, and Spanish.

How much does a counselling session cost?

Individual counselling is $135 per hour. Vibroacoustic therapy and sound healing are $135 per session (1.5 hours). Couples sound healing is $185. The 5-session Individual Package is $600 and the Couples Package is $725. Workshops are priced on request.

What is vibroacoustic therapy?

Vibroacoustic therapy is a 3D inner-body sound massage targeting the muscular, skeletal, and nervous systems. It modulates the Vagus Nerve, triggering healing responses including reduced stress, improved sleep, and pain relief.

Do you support postpartum and prenatal clients?

Yes. Gloria provides specialised perinatal and postpartum support for women during pregnancy, birth recovery, and early motherhood — combining talking therapy with breathwork and body-based practices.

What languages are sessions available in?

Sessions are available in English, Portuguese, and Spanish.

Do you offer couples counselling?

Yes. Gloria offers couples counselling and couples sound healing in North Brisbane, helping partners improve communication, rebuild connection, and navigate life transitions together.

Do you accept NDIS funding?

Yes. Gloria is a registered NDIS provider and works with participants across all funding management types — NDIS-managed, self-managed, and plan-managed. Sessions can also be accessed through a GP Mental Health Treatment Plan (Management Plan). Please get in touch to discuss your specific plan and eligibility.
